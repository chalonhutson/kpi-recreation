ABOUT KPI

We engage citizens and policymakers with information to enact public policy solutions that protect the constitutional right to freedom of all Kansans, give them greater access to educational opportunities, and allow them to keep more of what they earn. By protecting freedom, we will improve everyone's quality of life, make Kansas more competitive, and attract new citizens and businesses.
